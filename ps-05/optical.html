How many triangles do you see?
Technically there are no triangles in this picture.
There are 3 circles with a pie wedge cut out.
Your eye wants to complete the triangle.
The spatially separated objects create an illusion of a triangle.
This is similar to a diagram called Kanizsa's Triangle.
